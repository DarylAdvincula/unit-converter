About the Area Converter

Convert between square meters, square feet, acres, hectares, and square miles. Commonly used for real estate listings, land surveying, and agricultural measurements.

How the conversion works

Area units are derived by squaring the corresponding length unit, so this converter is built on the same length ratios as the Length converter, applied twice. An acre and a hectare, however, are historical land-measurement units with their own fixed definitions rather than simple squares of a length unit.

Frequently asked questions

How many square feet are in an acre?
One acre equals 43,560 square feet, or about 4,046.86 square meters.
What's the difference between an acre and a hectare?
A hectare (10,000 square meters) is a metric unit, while an acre is an imperial/US customary unit. One hectare is about 2.47 acres.
How is square footage calculated for a house?
Square footage is typically length times width for each room, summed across all livable floor area — this converter can help translate that total into square meters or other units.
