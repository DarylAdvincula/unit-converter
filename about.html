About this site

Unit Converter is a free, independent tool built to make quick, accurate unit conversions available without clutter, sign-ups, or unnecessary tracking. It covers length, weight, temperature, volume, area, speed, time, and data storage — the conversions people look up most often in everyday life, cooking, travel, and work.

Who built this

This site is built and maintained by Kyle Daryl Advincula, an independent developer. It's a personal project, built from scratch with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no backend, no data collection beyond anonymized traffic analytics.

Why it exists

Most unit converters online are cluttered with excessive ads, slow load times, or unnecessary sign-up walls. This tool aims to do one thing well: give an accurate answer in a second or two, with enough context (formulas, common conversions, FAQs) to actually be useful, not just a bare input box.

Accuracy

All conversion factors used in this tool are based on internationally recognized standard definitions (SI units and officially defined imperial/US customary equivalents). Calculations run entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to a server.
