About the Time Converter

Convert between seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, and years. Handy for calculating durations, converting project timelines, or working out how many hours are in a given number of days.

How the conversion works

Time conversions are based on the second as the SI base unit, with each larger unit defined as a fixed multiple (60 seconds per minute, 3600 per hour, and so on). Note that 'month' here is approximated as 30 days and 'year' as 365 days, since calendar months and leap years vary.

Frequently asked questions

How many hours are in a week?
There are 168 hours in a week (7 days × 24 hours).
Why is a month approximated as 30 days?
Calendar months vary from 28 to 31 days, so this converter uses 30 days as a practical average for quick estimates. For exact calendar-based calculations, a dedicated date calculator is more precise.
How many seconds are in a day?
There are 86,400 seconds in a day (24 × 60 × 60).
