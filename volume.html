About the Volume Converter

Convert between liters, gallons, cups, milliliters, tablespoons, and teaspoons. Especially handy for following recipes written in a different measurement system, or converting fuel and liquid capacity figures.

How the conversion works

This converter uses the liter as its base unit. US customary volume units (cups, gallons, fluid ounces) are converted using their official US definitions — note that US and UK (imperial) volume units differ slightly, and this tool uses US measurements throughout.

Frequently asked questions

How many cups are in a liter?
One liter equals approximately 4.227 US cups.
Is a US gallon the same as a UK gallon?
No — a US gallon (3.785 liters) is smaller than a UK imperial gallon (4.546 liters). Always check which system a recipe or spec sheet is using.
How many tablespoons are in a cup?
There are 16 tablespoons in one US cup, and 3 teaspoons in one tablespoon.
