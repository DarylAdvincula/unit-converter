Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What this site does

Unit Converter is a free tool for converting between measurement units. All conversion calculations happen locally in your browser using JavaScript — your input values are never sent to a server or stored anywhere.

Data we collect

This site does not use cookies for tracking, does not require an account, and does not collect the values you enter into the converter. We may use privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand overall traffic patterns like page views and general geographic region — this data is anonymized and not tied to individual visitors or their conversion inputs.

Advertising

This site may display advertisements served by third-party networks (such as Google AdSense). These networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads based on your visit to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or via Google's Ad Settings.

Third-party links

This site does not knowingly link to third-party services beyond font and analytics providers. If that changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed via the contact information listed on this site's GitHub repository.
