About the Weight Converter

Convert between kilograms, pounds, ounces, stone, grams, and metric tonnes. Useful for cooking, shipping, fitness tracking, or converting a doctor's chart from one system to another.

How the conversion works

This converter uses the kilogram as the base unit, since it's the SI standard for mass. Pounds, ounces, and stone are converted using their internationally agreed definitions (1 pound = 0.45359237 kg exactly), so results stay accurate even at large or very small values.

Frequently asked questions

How many pounds are in a kilogram?
One kilogram equals approximately 2.20462 pounds. This exact ratio (1 lb = 0.45359237 kg) has been the international standard since 1959.
What is a stone in weight?
A stone is a British imperial unit equal to 14 pounds, or about 6.35 kilograms. It's still commonly used in the UK and Ireland for body weight.
Is mass the same as weight?
Technically no — mass measures the amount of matter in an object, while weight measures the force of gravity on it. But in everyday conversions like this one, the terms are used interchangeably since we're on Earth.
